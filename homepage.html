MAPPA Co., Ltd. (Japanese: 株式会社MAPPA, Hepburn: Kabushiki-gaisha MAPPA),
is a Japanese animation studio headquartered in Suginami, Tokyo.
Founded in 2011 by Madhouse co-founder and producer Masao Maruyama,
it has produced anime works including
The God of High School, Jujutsu Kaisen, Attack on Titan: The Final Season, and Chainsaw Man. MAPPA is an acronym for Maruyama Animation Produce Project Association.
